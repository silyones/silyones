## About

I build AI systems that actually work in production — not just notebooks that demo well.

Right now I'm at Ton Technology Services, building voice agent pipelines with ElevenLabs, Sarvam AI, LiveKit, and Twilio. We hit sub-2s end-to-end latency on real telephony workloads. Before that, at Samsung Innovation Campus, I worked on shrinking transformer models for edge hardware — 40% faster inference, 30% smaller, 98% accuracy retained.

My work sits across multi-agent orchestration, voice AI infrastructure, and autonomous developer tooling. I care about systems that are observable, resilient, and don't break at 2 AM.

Outside of building things, I lead AI/ML programming as GDG On Campus AI Co-Lead and serve as President of IEEE Women in Engineering. I co-organized NammaSuraksha with Karnataka State Police. I also volunteered at Agentic AI Day, a Guinness World Record event for the world's largest agentic AI hackathon — 1,900+ developers.

**Open to:** Agentic AI Engineering · Voice AI · ML Engineering · AI Research · LLM Infrastructure
