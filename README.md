## About

I'm an AI Engineer with production experience designing and shipping **Generative AI**, **LLM-powered**, and **Agentic** systems end-to-end. My current work sits at the intersection of multi-agent orchestration, voice AI infrastructure, and autonomous developer tooling — built with LangChain, LangGraph, FastAPI, and cloud-native deployment patterns.

At **Ton Technology Services**, I build production-grade voice agent pipelines integrating ElevenLabs, Sarvam AI, LiveKit, and Twilio — achieving sub-2s end-to-end latency on real telephony workloads. Previously at **Samsung Innovation Campus**, I optimized on-device transformer inference with INT8 quantization and structured pruning, achieving 40% faster inference with 30% size reduction at 98% accuracy retention.

I care about systems that actually work in production: observable, resilient, latency-aware, and cost-efficient. I bring the same rigor to multi-agent orchestration that I bring to TTS prompt engineering for noisy ASR inputs.

Beyond engineering, I lead AI/ML programming as **GDG On Campus AI Co-Lead**, serve as **President of IEEE Women in Engineering**, and co-organized the NammaSuraksha Hackathon with Karnataka State Police. I volunteered at the Agentic AI Day event that set a **Guinness World Record** for the world's largest agentic AI hackathon (1,900+ developers).

**Open To:** Agentic AI Engineering · Voice AI Engineering · ML Engineering · AI Research Engineering · LLM Infrastructure · Full-time & Contract roles (Bengaluru preferred, remote considered)
